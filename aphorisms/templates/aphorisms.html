Aphorisms for the Neurodivergent

THIS IS AWARENESS

Stop

Sit quietly

Ask for openness

Your job is to feel

Learn from breathing

Letting out is letting go of thinking

All things pass even to die

As best you can

Set an intention

Time slows

To the space of a breath

THIS IS ACCEPTANCE

the best I can do

is to take care of myself

at my worst

if all I can do

is to sit

lost in confusion

then so be it

if there is pain or fear

"Bring It On"

"I love pain"

"Pain sets me free"

ARRIVE AT AN ACTION

perform healing tasks

however small

experience

pleasure, relaxation, comfort

fullfill a longing

prioritize by doing

watching

what you do
